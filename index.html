About Me

I am a Geospatial Information Systems(GIS) professional currently living and working in Atlanta. I enjoy observing and analyzing data from a geospatial perspective. My interests include geoinformatics, remote sensing, web/software development and UI/UX design. I graduated from Georgia Southern University with a Bachelor's degree in Geology and a minor in GIS. 6 months after I graduated I enrolled in the Full Stack Flex Web Development program at Georgia Tech where I learned the fundamentals of both front-end and back-end web development. With my background in GIS and web development, I am interested in combining the two fields to create web maps that display all kinds of data.
